Apps for the things you actually do

The catalog: apps, live scenes, icon sets, cursors, and widgets. One click each.

Pick a job, a mood, or a look. Install it. Get back to work. The catalog updates live — new apps, scenes, widgets, icon sets, and cursor sets show up in the Shop without a plugin update.

Catalog installs are still checked before they move in: ODD expects HTTPS, supported content types, first-party catalog URLs, declared SHA-256 hashes, and unique slugs.
