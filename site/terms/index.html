ODD catalog service

Terms of service

The ODD catalog service at odd.regionallyfamous.com/catalog/v1/ provides static JSON, preview images, icons, and downloadable `.wp` bundles for the ODD WordPress plugin.

The catalog is provided as-is for use with ODD and WP Desktop Mode. Availability is not guaranteed, and catalog content may be updated, removed, or replaced as ODD evolves.

The plugin validates catalog rows before installing content. First-party catalog bundle, icon, and preview URLs are expected to use HTTPS and stay under the configured catalog base by default, with declared SHA-256 hashes for downloadable bundles.

The ODD plugin is licensed under GPLv2 or later. Catalog bundles may include their own source and license details inside each package. Users are responsible for deciding which catalog items to install on their WordPress sites.

The catalog service does not create accounts, process payments, or provide paid subscriptions. Automated abusive traffic, attempts to bypass platform protections, or attempts to use the service to distribute harmful content may be blocked.

Questions can be opened at github.com/RegionallyFamous/odd/issues.
